Quick Instructions

The objective of the game is to uncover as many diamonds as possible without triggering a hidden mine. Each safe pick increases your winnings, but hitting a mine ends the round instantly, and you lose your bet. The challenge is knowing when to stop and collect before you start being unlucky.

Steps to Play:

  1. Choose your bet amount (ranging from $0.20 to $1000).
  2. Tap any tile to reveal what’s beneath.
  3. Each diamond boosts your multiplier and potential reward.
  4. “Cash out” whenever you’re happy with your win, or keep trying for a higher payout.
  5. The payout bar above the grid displays your current and upcoming multipliers.
